Full Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Praxis ("the App") is a meditation scheduler designed to help you plan and track your daily Vipassana meditation sittings. This Privacy Policy explains how the App works and how your information is handled.

2. No Data Collection

Praxis is a completely local app.

  • All meditation schedules, session history, and preferences are stored locally on your device.
  • We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on remote servers.
  • We do not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or trackers.

3. Information You Provide

Any information you enter into the App (for example, meditation times, session completions, or preferences) remains only on your device. The developer cannot access, view, or share this information.

4. Permissions

The App may request permissions strictly necessary to provide its features. Examples include:

  • Notifications: Used to deliver meditation reminders. These are not used for tracking.
  • Local storage: Used to save your schedules and session history.

5. Children’s Privacy

Because Praxis does not collect personal data, it is suitable for users of all ages. Parents or guardians should supervise users under 13 when configuring reminders or schedules.

6. Security of Your Data

All data stored by Praxis stays on your device. To help protect your data, we recommend using device-level security such as a passcode, biometric lock, or secure account on your device.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. If substantive changes are made, the Effective Date at the top of this page will be updated. You are encouraged to review this policy periodically.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact:

vipassana.meditation.for.all@gmail.com

Our mission is to support your Vipassana practice while respecting your privacy. Thank you for using Praxis.

Quick facts

Designed for simplicity and privacy-first usage.

  • No accounts, no cloud backups by default.
  • Data is exported only if you explicitly use an export feature (if available).
  • If you delete the app or its local data, your meditation history will be removed from the device.

Recommended security

Keep your device secure to protect your local data.

  1. Enable device lock (PIN/Password/Biometric).
  2. Use device backups only if you want to store copies of app data externally.
